AI Use With Program Pipeline

Along with our chatbot Will, our scholars will utilize various AI tools at their respective grade levels.

Grades 5 and 6

Students will utilize an adaptive learning platform CK-12 which adjusts to the individual students' needs and learning styles. This provides personalized lessons and recommendations, allowing students to learn at their own pace with an increased focus on areas where they need more assistance.

Grade 7

Scholars will make use of AI-assisted research and information retrieval through Elicit. AI has the power to gather information from various sources, analyze data, and extract insights. This assists greatly in a student's search for relevant resources.

Grade 8

Through the use of AI-assisted language learning , students will gain personalized language exercises, pronunciation feedback, and language practices through chatbots or virtual language tutors. The BuffaloPrep team utilizes Duolingo for AI-assisted language learning.

Grades 9, 10, 11, 12

Students can employ AI-driven data analysis through Polymer to analyze large datasets, identify patterns, and draw conclusions. This can be applied to various subjects like mathematics, science, social sciences, and economics.

Alumni Prep

Students will utilize all previous AI tools as they journey onto their post-secondary education and beyond. These tools will be available to all alumni and their progress will be saved.
